Things I Like


Music

What is music?

Vocal or instrumental sounds (or both) combined in such a way as to produce beauty of form, harmony,
and expression of emotion.

Why do I like this?

Music is an art. Beauty can be heard and it makes you feel a certain way. For me, the reason I love music
is because of this reason, the beauty of sounds expression emotions and feelings through the use of instruments
or vocals that people make.

My personal favorites?

My favorite song of all time is Creep by Radiohead, the emotions I feel everytime I hear it speaks to me. The
longing for something you want but you can't have it, it hurts. When you wanted love and affection but instead what
they returned was judgement of your character because of how you're different from them. I often sing this song
when I feel disappointed at myself and whenever sing this song, I sing it would often find myself singing this song
with an intense desire to sing about how I feel until I feel better.

My favorite band, suprisingly, is not Radiohead but Nirvana. They are the one band that inspired and motivated
me to know music more. They tought me so many things, how to sing with passion, not being afraid to be different,
be confident of yourself even if others put you down, appreciate simplicity than complexity and more.




Games

What is game?

A form of play or sport, especially a competitive one played according to rules and decided by skill,
strength, or luck.

Why do I like this?

Game is an art. Experiencing another world through playing is a different experience than reality. The
difference is that, in game you can be anything that you are not in reality. Games make you feel you can be best
than others, you can be different from how you really are, you can feel special, you can do anything you want.
Feeling that, you are having excitement, curiousity and more is what makes game feel so good.

My personal favorites?

My favorite game of all time is Minecraft, the endless possiblity of what you can do in game is so cool. You can
do anything there, build a kingdom, make it functional, create contraptions, tell a story using those. Some say that it is a
child's game for having such aesthetic but it can be said to other games too, it just depends if you enjoy it or no. For me
I loved the game since I was a young child and having a chance to grow up playing it is such an awesome experience.

My most played game is League of Legends, time I have spent with my friends in this game are irreplacable fun
times, that I sometimes wish I can go back to. Others may have said that this game is the most toxic game and has the
most toxic community but games are suppose to be enjoyed and if thats how they enjoy playing it then it's not bad but
it's bad to ruin others fun. For me, having fun is if you spend those moments with your friends. Most of my friends
were playing this game so I joined them.




Anime

What is anime?

A style of Japanese film and television animation, typically aimed at adults as well as children.

Why do I like this?

Anime is an art. Stories being told is like you are being involved, anime makes it so that the story is animated.
A journey about pirates, love, fighting giant robots, anime most likely has it all. Countless stories being told this way
is just astounding to me.

My personal favorites?

My favorite anime of all time is Your Lie in April. This anime has changed my view how I should see the world,
how I see love works, how life can be in the most unexpected ways, and it inspired me to play music to the point I
asked for a keyboard/piano but I was given a guitar. Because of this anime, I'm a sucker for a story with tragedy, a
love that isn't suppose to happen so something sad happens.
