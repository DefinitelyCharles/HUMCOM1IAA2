Genres

Shounen

  • "For boys"
  • e.g. My Hero Academia, Dragon Ball Z, Slam Dunk

Shoujo

  • "Young girls"
  • e.g. Sailor Moon, Nana, Marie Antoinette's Versailles

Seinen

  • Robots, Action, Magic, and Warfare
  • e.g. Black Lagoon, Haven't You Heard? I'm Sakamoto, One-Punch Man

Isekai

  • Entirely around ordinary people being whisked away to fantasy worlds
  • e.g. No Game No Life, That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime, Re: Zero

Mecha

  • Giant robot
  • e.g. Mobile Suit Gundam, Darling in the FRANXX, Full Metal Panic!

Slice of Life

  • Stories that observe everyday life as it is lived by an individual or group of characters.
  • e.g. K-On!, Ms. Kobayashi's Dragon Maid, Wotakoi: Love Is Hard for Otaku
