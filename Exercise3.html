How to change guitar string


This is a simple guide on how to change your guitar string.
For more information about guitars please click here.

Step 1: Prepare your tools and materials

    Required:

    • 1 Pack of Guitar string
      • You need new strings to replace the old strings.
      A standard guitar requires 6 strings.
    • Tuner
      • Your ear's perfect pitch
      Guitar tuner device
      Guitar tuner application

    Optional:

    • Guitar Pick
    • String Winder
    • String Cutter
    • Pliers
    • Cleaning Cloth
    • Cleaning Brush
    • Lemon Oil

Step 2: Loosen/cut and remove the strings

Suggested tools:

  • String Winder (optional)
  • String Cutter (optional)

There are 2 way to get the old strings out of the guitar.

  1. Loosen the string
    2. Safest way to remove the string.
    Takes a bit longer depending in how much of the strings you left in at the head of guitar.
  2. Cut the string
    3. Fastest way to remove the string.
    Might whip you depending on where you cut it.
    Since it is now cutted in half, remove the left over strings at the head of guitar.

Note: Slowly and carefully remove the string from the guitar. Never rush or else string might break
without any warning and whip you to where it can reach


Step 3: Remove the steel or plastic end of the string

Suggested tools

  • Pliers (optional)

Remove the steel or plastic end of the string slowly. Doing so will ensure less posibility of the whole or the board of the guitar to be damaged.

Note: If the end of the string is somehow stuck, please don't force it out and use a pliers.


Step 3: Clean the fretboard (optional)

Suggested tools:

  • Cleaning Brush
  • Cleaning Cloth

Use the cleaning brush to slowly scrub off any hard dirt that is attacked in the guitar's fretboard.

Use the cleaning cloth to slowly rub off any marks from the surface of the guitar's fretboard.


Step 4: Oil the fretboard (optional)

Suggested tools:

  • Lemon Oil
  • Cleaning Cloth

The citrus of lemon oil will help and ensure the fretboard be clean and shiny at the same time.

To do so, apply oil on a clean part of the cleaning cloth and rub it on the surface of the fretboard.


Step 5: Insert new strings

Suggested tools:

  • Pack of Guitar String

Slowly insert it the guitar string in the guitar's bridge. After the string gets through the guitar's bridge, gently pull the string to guitar's head and insert them on the guitar's tuning keys.

Ensure that the string wont move out and it would change from its designated position in the guitar's nut. Wrap and wind the strings on the guitar's tuning key.


Step 6: Tune the guitar

Suggested tools:

  • Tuner
  • Guitar Pick (Optional)

Tune your guitar into standard tuning which is eADGBE.
